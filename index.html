Wheel of Time Book Series

By Robert Jordan

What is Wheel of Time?

The Wheel of Time is a fantasy book series written by Robert Jordan. There are thirteen total in the series, not including the prequel. The story follows Rand al'Thor, Perrin Aybara, Mat Cauthon, Egwene al'Vere, and Nynaeve al'Meara as they must leave the only place they have ever known when evil forces are after them. It is a story about heros and villians, triumphs and defeat, love and loss. It is a huge commitment with over 4 million words and thousands of characters throughout the long series. However, it is a classic in the genre for a reason, and that reason being it is an amazing story that will have you fell every emotion. As you may tell by my quick summary, this is one of my favorite book series. It has even surpassed Harry Potter in my rankings which I did not think possible.
